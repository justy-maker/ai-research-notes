## 轉錄內容

Did you know that when a buffalo sends a storm coming at them that they run towards the storm? They know that the faster they move towards the storm, the less time they'll spend in the storm. And that what you might be doing is running away from the storm, which means as it comes at you, you stay in it and you stay in it and you're backing up trying to avoid it when if you just like attack it, you would literally get through it quicker and it might hurt. On the back side of that, you'll be better. You'll be way stronger. You'll have more resilience and you'll build a new level of confidence that you didn't even know was there.
