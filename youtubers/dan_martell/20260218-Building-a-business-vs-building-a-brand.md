# Building a business vs building a brand

- **頻道**：Dan Martell
- **平台**：youtube
- **時長**：0 分鐘
- **來源**：https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V-OZ5ik5Q44
- **轉錄方式**：subtitle

## 轉錄內容

Don't just build a business, build a brand. Building a business is just selling product. Having a brand builds trust. Think about it. $3 t-shirt versus $300 t-shirt versus $3,000 t-shirt. What's the difference? The product's the same. Brand pricing power. You want to be a millionaire? Yeah. Build a business. You want to be a billionaire? You have to build a brand.
