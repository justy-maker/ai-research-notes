# 5 tips to prompt AI like a Pro

- **平台**：youtube

## 轉錄內容

Five tips to get better AI responses that nobody's teaching you. Tip number one, start with the outcome. Before you tell AI what to do, you got to tell it why you're doing it. The more it knows about the specific goal, the better your answers are going to be. Tip number two, use your voice. Just use any voice to text app like Whisper Flow or Super Whisper, and it'll make it really easy for you. Tip number three, ask it to ask you questions. At the end of every prompt, you say, "Ask me any questions you need to give me a better answer." Tip number four, create system prompts. If you're chatting with AI and it finally works, ask it to create a system prompt so you don't have to explain yourself again. Tip number five, stop using Chad GPT for everything. There's a ton of AI tools out there and each one is good at a specific thing. Chad GPT isn't really the best at any one of them.
