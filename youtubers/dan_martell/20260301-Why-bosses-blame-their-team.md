## 轉錄內容

Why do bosses blame their team and not themselves? >> Whoing hired them? Who trained them? You blame somebody on your team when there's a mistake. You didn't train them. You didn't create a dashboard for them. You didn't tell them what greatness looks like. Somebody makes a mistake on my team. First thing I ask myself is, how did I contribute to this? Then I asked myself, is there a process that they were supposed to follow to achieve this outcome? If the answer is no, bad on me. Then I asked myself, if there was a process and they followed it, but it didn't get the result, did I properly train them? Now, if both of those answers are yes and I know I don't have a process problem, I have a people problem. Bosses blame their team.
